# Performance Review — 2023

Source: Annual performance review (Envase Technologies / WiseTech Global transition period)

## Achievements — Exceeds Expectations

Dan has been successfully leading the development of several projects and has demonstrated great ability as a leader. He ensures that everyone in the team understands the scope of each of the stories taking the front role during backlog refinement. He has successfully delivered each of the features he has worked on in the past year.

Dan is always looking for ways in which to improve the process. He participates in retrospectives providing useful feedback and defining action items to help the team become more efficient.

He has been critical in the design and architecture of Envase Core API, as well as the new installer technology. He was deeply involved in the initial stages of Envase Tracer and lead the implementation of the management side of the system. He then moved into the implementation of the new installer technology that will be delivered this year.

His knowledge of our systems, our products, and the industry make him a very valuable resource. He is quick to help other teams integrating our services. He monitors discussions in Teams channels and jumps into conversations when help is needed. He has done an enormous work helping the Profit Tools EDI team with many of their requests in the past year.

Dan has help with the interview and hiring process of new members of the team. He has also participated in the on-boarding process, and he is constantly mentoring other developers and automation engineers. He indoctrinates them to follow good practices and to strive for quality.

## Problem Solving — Meets Expectations

Confronted with a problem, Dan likes to look at different solutions. He is good evaluating whether a solution will meet the requirements, but he sometimes hesitates to decide. He is knowledgeable and capable to make the decision, but he tends to doubt himself. He should trust his instincts while keeping an eye open for potential problems.

Dan has a strong background in software design, and he is constantly improving himself or helping others improve. He likes to learn new technologies and systems, so that he can later apply that knowledge.

Dan needs to be less afraid of failure. He needs to try new ideas and be willing to throw away solutions if it becomes obvious they are not going to work. He should start decomposing problems into smaller ones and solve them individually instead of focusing solely on the big picture, which may obscure the end goal.

## Communication — Meets Expectations

Dan has excellent communication skills and the ability to communicate with different groups within the organization. He can explain concepts to less technological people and at the same time, he can dive into deep tech conversations with the development team. He is concise and always makes sure his message is clear and understood.

He is able to communicate clearly with everyone in the team. This is always a challenge because the different cultures and levels of understanding. He is patient with the team, listens and asks questions for clarification, and ensures everyone understands him when needed.

## Creativity & Adaptability — Meets Expectations

Dan has no problem changing the projects that he works on. He likes to try new technologies and get involved in any of the opportunities our team has. He adapts well and fast, and he is able to contribute right from the beginning of a project.

Dan understands that requirements change, and he is always willing to adapt to those changes. He likes to be involved in the discussions, and he provides excellent positive and negative feedback when needed.

Dan has ample experience in the industry and with Profit Tools. This experience sometimes gets in the way of creativity. He is open to try new solutions, but it takes him longer to understand those solutions because of the many preconceptions he has. Still, his feedback is invaluable.

## Culture — Exceeds Expectations

Many people that works with Dan have told me about how much they like to work with him, and how much they learn from him. He is also a great team leader that ensures everyone in the team is brought up to speed on the goals of the project. He respects everyone's individuality, but also sees the value of teamwork.

Dan is always respectful with others regardless of their culture. We have a diverse group of people in our team, and he has a great relationship with all of them. He respects and is well respected by others.

Dan is very proud of his work, and he doesn't hesitate to speak the truth even when the truth might not be favorable. I can completely trust his statements, and I value his integrity.

Like I said before, Dan has a deep connection with everyone he interacts in the organization, and he is very well respected professionally and personally. He cares for the people he works with, and he's always available to help.

Quality is the main goal in everything Dan does. He's always looking for ways to improve our products and processes to achieve excellence.

- Excellent communication across technical and non-technical audiences
